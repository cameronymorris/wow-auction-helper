Welcome back!

New functionality have been added since your last visit, among them youl'l find the following:

Watchlist
  • Allows you to add items with an alert value. If the item is found below or equal that value you will recieve a push notification, showing how many items meet your criteria.
  • If you have a recipe for an item, the crafting cost will be visible also, and you will get notifications if the item has more than 10% potential profit.
  • By default, youl'l get a pre-defined list. Feel free to make it your own!; edit the groups, remove groups add items etc.
  • When deleting a group you can move it's items to another group or delete the items and group.
  • Keep in mind, that you need to have the webapp open for notifications to work. You do not need to have it in the foreground/focus however. You can play a game, or do something else while the app will notify you in the background!

This message will only be displayed upon feature updates and not bug fixes. If you find any bugs or wish to give feedback on the app, please do so on in the reddit thread. As always, any feedback is appreciated!
